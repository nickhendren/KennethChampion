About Me


My name is Kenneth Champion and I am an independent author of epic fantasy novels. I have lived my whole life loving to get immersed in the fantasy worlds created by authors everyone knows and loves. Now I have the opportunity to create my own stories, and it is a dream come true.

When I am not fulfilling my love for writing I am spending quality time with close friends, my family, my dog, and of course when I'm bored I watch netflix - like a lot of netflix.

It Rained Upon The Arena

Nick Bint is a seventeen year old who has trained everyday of his life in order to become an honored Refect in the land of Hentrio when he would finally come of age. The Refect serve the king and guard Hentrio’s cities in order to keep its citizens safe. With so many gifted individuals who can cast destructive magic or master swordsmanship in a short amount of time; violent crime is a relentless problem. Nick wanted nothing more than to do his part and protect the good citizens of Hentrio…

However, through a slew of ill-fated events of one night, Nick finds himself with a bloody dagger in his right hand. He stands still as blood drips to the floor beneath him. The Refect storm in, put Nick's hands behind his back, and arrest him. Nick is sentenced to two years in the Vincot City Arena. Every citizen fears a sentence in the arena because only the greatest of warriors can survive past six months.

Nick must now fight for his life in front of the lively crowds of the arena, day in, and day out. Every inmate within the cells below the arena is a potential combatant to fight to the death with. He will have to face the harsh mental and physical torment the arena has to offer. The sands of the arena are the foundation of the epic quest Hentrio has in store for young Nick.

They Were Born Upon Ashes

The enemy destroys lives and plans on taking over Hentrio - all the while playing a torturous game with the king's men.

The six have taken Genold City and their leader Vicon sits on the golden throne in Castle Teon. Thousands of people are removed from their homes and everyday lives as they try to escape the mad rule the six plan to implement in Genold City. Nick Bint and a few thousand men, women, and children follow the king’s men as they begin their epic quest to the city of Ralton in the north.

However, there are no safe roads or guarantees in Hentrio. As winter approaches fast, the danger only increases. Food and warmth will become a top priority. Meanwhile, Nick must search within himself to master the art of becoming a supreme Reza. He will have to learn how to use the ancient and long forgotten spells to help the king’s men and their mission. There will be dragons, battles, and magic. The incredible quest of Nick Bint and the king’s men begins here.

Dragons Descended Upon The Wicked

War is inevitable. The six must be stopped.

Nick Bint finds himself in the mysterious old lady's cabin named Dricos, which is located just outside of the dragon's lair. In just a few short hours he has to walk into the dragon's lair, which will be filled with thousands of dragons in search for his dragon born. His dragon born is the dragon that was born at the exact moment he was. As a supreme Reza, Nick has the ability to command and mount his dragon just as the villainous six have already done.

Meanwhile, the camp must try to make a bold move to leave the north bend and travel further north to the hostile city of Terrel, in order to survive the unbearably cold winter months ahead. If they are to succeed, then it will not be the cold and scarce amounts of food that will kill them. However, the six are not done tormenting the refugee camp just yet. Nick and the king's men have to muster all recourses and plan diligently if they want to defeat their enemy.

Glory Beyond Battle
The Complete Series

Nick Bint is a seventeen year old Paplon who believes that he has his life perfectly planned out in the world of Hentrio. The Reza race are experts at casting destructive magic, while the Paplon are gifted in weaponry combat. But, through a series of ill-fated events Nick finds himself in the Vincot City Arena. While he battles for his life he learns that he has powers that flow through his veins that were thought to be long extinct centuries ago.

With his newfound powers Nick must embark on an epic adventure throughout Hentrio in order to stop the sinister group known as the six who plan to take over the golden throne, and then the world. Nick teams up with the king's men who teach him how to control his new abilities and use them to help the innocent civilians who's lives were ruined by the six's mad rule. One such ability is being able to mount a dragon and command them through ancient dragon tongue.

Nick Bint is called upon to be the unlikely hero to save Hentrio from a tyranical rule. But, will he prevail or fall to an untimely demise?
